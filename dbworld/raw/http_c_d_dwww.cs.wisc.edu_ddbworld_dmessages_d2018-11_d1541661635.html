========= Call for Papers ========

** About OJIS
Open Journal of Information Systems (OJIS), an open access and peer-reviewed online journal, publishes original and creative research results on information technologies. OJIS publishes its articles under the open access model. All articles of OJIS are fully open access and online available to readers free of charge. Accepted manuscripts are published online immediately. OJIS aims to provide a forum for sharing and exchanging ideas, experiences and research results among scientists and practitioners of information technologies. OJIS publishes regular research papers, short communications, reviews and visionary papers in all aspects of information systems. There is no restriction on the length of the papers.

** Scope
The journal expects original, high-quality papers in all aspects of information technologies.

** Indexing: 
OJIS is indexed by DBLP and other bibliographic databases and libraries, and a full digital copy of the content of this journal is permanently preserved in the German National Library ( https://www.ronpub.com/OJIS/indexing ).

** Open-Access Publication Fees:
All articles of OJIS are fully open access and online available to readers free of charge. In order to support open access, OJIS has to charge a one-time publication fee for each accepted article. Please visit the journal webpage for more information ( https://www.ronpub.com/OJIS/publication-fees ).

** Preparation & Submission
OJIS welcomes submissions at any time and accepted manuscripts are published online immediately. The preparation and submission of manuscripts should follow the author guidelines (http://www.ronpub.com/OJIS/author-guidelines).
