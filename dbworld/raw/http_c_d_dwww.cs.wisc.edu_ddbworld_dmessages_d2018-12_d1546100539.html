Doctoral Researcher/University Assistant in Multidisciplinary Design & User Research
Vienna University of Technology (TU Wien)

Application Deadline: 17th January 2019

** Description **
The Multidisciplinary Design & User Research (MDUR, E 193-04) Group at the Insitute of Visual Computing and Human-Centered Technology (TU Wien, Austria) is looking for a doctoral researcher/university assistant (30 hours/week) for two years. The position provides the opportunity for development and submission of a PhD thesis to TU Wien as well as involvement in research and teaching activities of the research unit.

** Qualifications **
We are looking for a candidate with a Master degree in computer science or in an area which is connected to people-technology-design disciplines (like HCI, user centered design, CSCW, design including software- and social science-related design methodologies, socially embedded computing, social informatics or similar areas).

- Profound knowledge of and interest in one or more of the following research areas, which are connected to design and the use of information and communication technologies (ICTs), are required: tangible user interface design, interaction design, usability, assisted living technologies, social networks and communication through digital media, computer-supported collaboration, social impact of ICTs, organisational informatics, knowledge management, change management. 

- Knowledge and/or experience in carrying out qualitative empirical user studies
- Knowledge and/or experience in writing research project proposals, together with colleagues of the research unit
- Good communication and social skills for team work
- Very good in German and English 

In addition, you have:
- interest in empirical studies, practical design aspects, and theoretical-conceptual investigations
- interest in identification and writing of research questions and results as well as their presentation
- curiosity and enthusiasm for the areas described above 

** Terms of employment **
The appointment will be based on the Austrian Collective Agreement for university staff. The period of employment is 24 months. The workplace will be TU Wien, Favoritenstrasse 9-11/193-04, A-1040 Vienna, Austria. The position is a 30 hours/week position for a pre-doc university assistant for two years, starting February 1st 2019. The monthly minimum salary is currently (in the collective agreement ¡ì49 (3) Pay Grade B1) EUR 2.096,60 gross (14 times/year). 

** Application **
Applications including 
- a cover letter, 
- the motivation for this position,
- Curriculum Vitae, 
- the abstract, the table of context, and the summary of the master thesis,
- list of publications, patents, ... (if any),
- one or two reference letters (from academic context),
- certificates, grade transcripts (with translation if not in English or German)
should be submitted as a single PDF file via email to susanne.pietsch-brousek@tuwien.ac.at (Service Unit of HR Administration - Scientific Staff). Further information may be obtained from Prof. Hilda Tellioglu (hilda.tellioglu@tuwien.ac.at).
